# Freemium Model

## Gate Strategy
AI is the primary paywall. Core tracking works fully on free tier within quantity limits.

## Free Tier
- Pillars: up to 4
- Goals: up to 5 active
- Habits: up to 7
- AI interactions: 10/day (coaching + quick-add)
- Templates: all, no AI customization
- Journal: full access, no AI referencing
- Reviews: weekly only, manual
- Calendar sync: read-only
- History: 30 days
- Life Map: full access

## Pro ($8–12/mo)
- Pillars: up to 8
- Goals: unlimited
- Habits: unlimited
- AI: unlimited (full decomposition, template customization, analysis)
- Templates: all with AI customization
- Journal: full with AI-powered insights
- Reviews: weekly + monthly with AI summaries and pillar breakdown
- Calendar sync: two-way
- History: unlimited + export
- Life Map: full access

## Pricing Rationale
Sunsama: $20/mo, no free tier. TickTick Pro: $36/year. Todoist Pro: $5/mo. At $8–12/mo, Momentum undercuts Sunsama while offering AI features none of the cheaper tools have.
