# Positioning & Differentiation

## One-Liner
Momentum is the AI-native goal tracker that talks to you — tell it what you want to achieve, and it structures your life pillars, goals, habits, and tasks into a coherent system, then coaches you through execution.

## Target User
- **Primary**: Ambitious individuals (25–40) who set goals but lose structure after the first week. They want a system that connects daily actions to who they want to become.
- **Secondary**: Productivity enthusiasts who rotate through tools and would stick with one that actively helps them think and reflect.
- **Anti-target**: Teams, enterprises, project managers. Momentum is personal-first.

## Unique Position
The only app combining Life Pillars (identity-level framing), AI conversational goal creation, habit tracking with streaks, micro-journaling, and daily coaching — in a native cross-platform app with a free tier.

## Closest Competitor
Griply — has Vision → Life Areas → Goals hierarchy but no AI, no native desktop app, no journal integration.
